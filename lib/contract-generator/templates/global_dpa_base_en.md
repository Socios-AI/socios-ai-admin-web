# GLOBAL DATA PROCESSING ADDENDUM - BASE TEMPLATE

This Data Processing Addendum (the **DPA**) forms part of the Agreement when either Party processes personal data in connection with the software, platform, partnership, customer onboarding, support, marketing, billing, analytics, AI operations, or related services.

## 1. Roles

For account creation, billing, and platform administration, the Company acts as an independent controller of its own business records. For customer/end-user data, partner CRM/leads, and AI prompts, inputs, and outputs processed on behalf of the Receiving Party, the Company acts as processor and the Receiving Party acts as controller, unless the Parties agree otherwise in writing.

## 2. Data Categories

Account and billing data, customer/end-user contact and transactional data, leads and CRM records, and AI prompts, inputs, and outputs submitted through the platform.

## 3. Purposes of Processing

Providing, operating, securing, and supporting the Sócios AI platform and the commercial partnership described in the Agreement, including customer onboarding, support, billing, analytics, and AI-assisted automation.

## 4. Security Measures

The Company shall implement reasonable technical and organizational measures appropriate to the nature of the processing, including access controls, encryption in transit where applicable, authentication, logging, vendor controls, incident response, and separation of environments as applicable.

## 5. Subprocessors

Subprocessors: available upon request.  
Objection process: as set forth in the Company's subprocessor policy.

## 6. International Transfers

Applicable modules: as required by the counterparty's jurisdiction under this Agreement's Country-Specific Addenda.

## 7. Data Subject Requests

The Parties shall reasonably cooperate to respond to valid data subject requests where legally required.

## 8. Incident Notification

Security incident notification period: without undue delay and in any event within seventy-two (72) hours of becoming aware.

## 9. AI Model Training and Improvement

The Company shall not use customer personal data submitted through the platform to train or improve models for third parties without the applicable notice, legal basis, opt-out/consent mechanism, and customer controls.
